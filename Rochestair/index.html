Rochestair

Welcome to the Rochestair, an airshow featuring the world famous USAFA Thunderbirds! Each summer The Rochestair Airshow pulls in a good number of airshow enthusiasts from all around New York to watch the best airshow the East Coast offers. The Rochestair Airshow provides viewers with an avenue to celebrate patriotism, adventure, and technology through the dozens of airborne performers and their incredible acrobatic feats. Sponsored by Greater Rochester International airport, the Rochestair is a family-friendly, inspiring event that thrills people of all age groups.
